About Us

JK’s Restaurant: A Fusion of Exquisite Dining, Cozy Rooms, and Leisure Facilities.

JK’s Restaurant was founded in 2023 by Pranav and Kavya, a couple who share a passion for food and hospitality. They wanted to create a place where guests can enjoy a variety of cuisines, relax in comfortable rooms, and have fun in the swimming area. Their goal is to provide a memorable experience for every guest who visits their restaurant.
